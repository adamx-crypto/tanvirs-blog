Hello people, welcome to my blog!!, I am Tanvir Siddqe Leion, I live in Dhaka, Bangladesh, and I love to travel to different kinds of places, meet new people and taste new foods.

I always travel whenever I get spare time

I have visited many districts and villages of Bangladesh, and also abroad.

One of my best trips in Bangladesh are:

  • Cox's Bazar
    • Pretty much everyone knows about Cox's Bazar, the breeze of the wind is awesome when you stand by the sea shore.
  • Sylhet
    • The tea gardens of Sylhet are amazing, you can enjoy a cup of tea there with the peaceful moment of nature
  • Comilla
    • I went to the border which is located between Bangladesh and India, the place is very greenish and very peaceful.

    In Dhaka:

  • Munshiganj
    • Munshiganj was the best among all, I really enjoyed the places around Munshiganj, the environment is very greenish, the people were very friendly.
  • Sonargaon
    • I saw many old historical buildings, items, and I met many people who still follow the old tradition
  • Mawa
    • The highway to Mawa is fabulous, Mawa is an awesome place for friendly hangouts, and you can enjoy tea and Hilsa fish over there.
  • LalBagh fort
    • The old buildings and the Royal Mansions look very aesthetic.
  • Keraniganj
    • I visited Shamol Bangla Resort at Keraniganj, the resort was amazing for family hangouts
  • Pangaon port
    • The Port of Pangaon is an inland port and container terminal on the Buriganga River in Dhaka District, Bangladesh.
